About Us

I am thrilled to introduce MARIE VIANCA DESIGN a cutting-edge interior design company committed to transforming spaces into unique and exquisite living art. As a founder and lead interior designer, I'm eager to collaborate with you to bring a fresh clear creative vision that will transform your home into something suited just for you.

MARIE VIANCA DESIGN we believe that true excellence lies in the details. We meticulously oversee every aspect of the design process ensuring that every element contributes seamlessly to the overall aesthetic and functionality of the space. From the selection of materials to the placement of the decor, our keen attention to detail sets us apart.

Our design philosophy revolves around pushing creative boundaries and thinking outside the conventional norms. We take pride in infusing each project with a distinctive and imaginative touch, ensuring that the spaces we design not only meet but exceed our clients' expectations. Creativity is the driving force behind every concept we bring to life.

Marie Vianca

Owner & Chief Creative Director

From the vibrant island of Puerto Rico, meet our Owner and Chief Creative Director, Marie Vianca Maldonado, who brings a unique blend of tropical flair, cultural richness, and timeless elegance to the world of interior design. With over 12 years of experience and a bachelor's degree in Interior Design, Marie has honed her craft with a passion for transforming spaces into personalized works of art that resonate with the soul.

She has working alongside industry veterans and honing her skills in prestigious design firms, Marie Vianca embarked on a journey of entrepreneurship, fueled by a desire to create a design studio that reflects her unique vision and values. Thus, her eponymous design firm was born – a sanctuary where creativity knows no bounds and every client's dream is treated with the utmost care and attention to detail.

Marie Vianca's approach to design is holistic and intuitive. She is not just an interior designer – she is a Dream Artisan, a storyteller, and a trusted partner in the journey to create spaces. Join her on a design adventure where dreams and interiors are transformed into exquisite living art.
